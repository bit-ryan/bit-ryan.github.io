My Carbon Foot Print Calculator

A carbon footprint calculator is like a special tool that helps you measure how much your actions affect the Earth. Imagine it's like a game where you put in things you do, like how you travel or what you eat, and it shows you how much pollution that creates. It's important because it helps us understand how our choices impact the planet. By using this calculator, we can learn how to make changes, like using less electricity or walking more, to reduce our carbon footprint and keep the Earth healthy for everyone. So, it's like a little helper that shows us how to be kinder to our planet!
